## Page 17

Pay Leverage
Leverage is a mathematical expression for the upside earning 
potential for a sales job. We express leverage as a multiplier of the 
target incentive
“The 3x Rule”
The amount of upside earnings related to the down side risks, on a 
“2-for-1” ratio; or 3x the at-risk component. 
For every $1 put at risk, $2 of upside earning potential are provided 
for outstanding performance
As a result, the more money at risk (high mix), more upside earning 
opportunities should be available.

## Page 27

Handling Clawbacks
● Apply clawbacks at a deal level, making it easy for the rep to 
follow what deals clawbacks have been applied to.
● Allow for a clear dispute process which makes it easy to follow 
the payout process for both the Finance and Sales Team.
● Automate this entire process for the business, taking the stress 
away from the ﬁnance and sales team — and promoting more 
trust on the sales commission process.
● Calculate the clawback at the multiple it was awarded at, a 
special and complex consideration if you have accelerators or 
tiers.
