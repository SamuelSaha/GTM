Problems with the funnel framework
The primary focus is on acquiring new
customers, often neglecting what happens after
the purchase. Once a sale is made, the funnel
ends.
Designed as linear, one-off purchases, which
doesn’t align well with modern subscription or
recurring revenue models (common in SaaS).
The classical funnel lacks mechanisms for
ongoing feedback and continuous relationship
nurturing after the sale.
The funnel doesn’t leverage existing customers
as growth drivers (e.g., referrals, expansions,
upsells).
